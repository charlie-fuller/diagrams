⚠️ Key Workshop Decisions

  • Technical Blueprinting REMOVED from account planning - moved to opportunity stage
  • Power Mapping SIMPLIFIED to C-Suite baseline only - detailed mapping at opportunity stage
  • Prompt Library NOT recommended - focus on conversational AI fluency ("cognitive shifts" training)
  • AE/BDR Division kept organic - agree at kickoff per account, AE accountable
  • Deep Research preferred over standard ChatGPT (~99% vs unreliable accuracy)
  • QA still required - "Trust but verify" even with AI assistance
  • Target: 70-80% automation - break into 10% incremental improvements
