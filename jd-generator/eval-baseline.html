Run 03
New failure: human checkpoint breach and conversation closure loop. The agent committed to Google Doc creation (turn 3) after a user clarification without re-presenting the full updated draft — checkpoint score 3/10. After the doc workaround resolved, the agent repeated “talk soon” and “I’ll be here” five times across turns 13–17 after the user had clearly ended the conversation, forcing an explicit callout. Conversational Skill dropped to 6.7, the lowest dimension score across all runs.
