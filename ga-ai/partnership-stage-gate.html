G&A AI Partnership

Stage-gate, end to end

Four stages, three gates. Stages say what the work is. Gates say what has to be true before we move forward.

Stage 01
Discover
Are they ready, and are we the right ones to help?
  • Intake triage and portfolio scoring
  • Readiness assessment across four dimensions
  • Remediation path for weak areas — no straight shot to Build
  • Sponsorship on record at exec, manager, and user level
A
Gate A · readiness
Before we build anything
  • Four readiness dimensions clear — technical, domain, strategic, data
  • Sponsor, manager, and user engagement confirmed
  • Named process owner who can decide, not just relay
  • Working Agreement signed between engager and partner
Stage 02
Build & Coach
Ship the agreed agents and grow builders inside the department so we can leave.
  • Build the agreed agents on the platform
  • Coach in-department builders toward independence
  • Co-author the adoption plan with the partner team
B
Gate B · handoff
Before we move to prove
  • Agreed agents delivered and performing
  • In-department builders operating independently
  • Adoption plan signed — owner, metric, cadence, escalation path
Stage 03
Prove
Show the work lands in practice — usage against the plan, not just at launch.
  • Run enablement — workshops, coaching, reinforcement
  • Track usage signals against the adoption plan's metric
  • Monthly reviews for the first 90 days, then quarterly
C
Gate C · closure
Before we hand over and step back
  • Closure memo signed by sponsor and process owner
  • Ownership transferred — builder and adoption owner in place
  • Adoption plan in operation — cadence running on its own
Stage 04
Scale
Keep it running without us. Feed the learning back into the platform.
  • Quarterly check-ins against the adoption metric
  • Harden the agents; fold patterns into the platform
  • Case study and methodology back into the Build System
